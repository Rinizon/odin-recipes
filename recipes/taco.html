Taco Recipe

Description

This tastes very close to the taco seasoning favored by a very popular fast-food taco restaurant. You can make this at home, and let your family prepare tacos the way they like.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Tortilla
  • Pico

Steps

  1. Cook meat
  2. Heat tortillas
  3. Shred cheese
  4. Assemble ingredients
